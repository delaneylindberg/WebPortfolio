Here you can find some examples of Delaney's film and audio works.

Black Lives Matter Analysis

Delaney used found footage to compile a narrative story detailing the accounts of the Black Lives Matter movement. Highlighting the lack of true freedom in our American society, Delaney attempts to demonstrate the message of the movement, while highlighting its power.

Anxiety (trigger warning*)

In this audio piece, Delaney captures the auditory experience of having a panic attack in a public setting. Basing the piece on her own experience of sensory overload, the audio experience replicates the overwhelming sounds and feelings associated with a panic attack.

*The audio in this piece attempts to capture the real-lived sounds and feelings of a panic attack. Please note that the listening experience can be overwhelming or triggering for listeners and listen at your own risk.
