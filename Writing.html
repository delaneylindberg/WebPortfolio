Here you can find some examples of Delaney's academic writing.

Memoir Sample

My Experiences with Anxiety

In the short memoir My Experiences with Anxiety, Delaney details her journey learning to understand and cope with anxiety. She elaborates upon her early experiences with panic attacks and how she continues to grow through and overcome mental struggle.

Literature Review Sample

LGBTQ+ Experiences in Catholic Schools

In the literature review LGBTQ+ Experiences in Catholic Schools, Delaney connects themes across published literature relating to the experiences of LGBTQ+ identifying students and teachers in Catholic school environments.

Narrative Journalism Sample

Domestic Abuse and the Importance of Education

In the narrative journalism piece Domestic Abuse and the Importance of Education, Delaney discusses a domestic violence story from her hometown to explain the necessity for domestic abuse education and awareness for young adults.
