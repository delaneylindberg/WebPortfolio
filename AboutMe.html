Here you can find some information about Delaney Lindberg and her academic and professional involvement.

Professional Experience

University of Pittsburgh, Pitt Pathfinder:

Delaney is currently a Pitt Pathfinder, a student tour guide for the University of Pittsburgh. In this role, she acts as the intermediary between prospective students and the university's Office of Admissions. Demonstrating a strength in communcation, Delaney uses her experience and knowledge to craft personalized tours for all visiting families.

Eberhart's Custom Embroidery, Department Supervisor:

From December of 2016 to June of 2021, Delaney worked at Eberhart's Custom Embroidery in Bensalem, PA. At her highest level in the company, she acted as a department supervisor, monitoring the design and printing of custom apparel.

Planet Fitness, Front Desk Associate:

During the summer of 2018, Delaney worked as a Front Desk Associate at Planet Fitness in Trevose, PA. Here, she demonstrating excelling costumer service and was tasked with implementing a training plan for new and current employees in positive customer service strategies.

Academic Experience

University of Pittsburgh (Pitt)

Delaney is currently a Junior at the University of Pittsburgh. She is pursuing a Bachelor of Arts of Communication Rhetoric in the Kenneth P. Dietrich School of Arts and Sciences, in addition to certificates in Digital Media and Leadership in the College of General Studies.

Outside of academics, Delaney is a Pitt Pathfinder (student tour guide) for the Office of Admissions. In addition, she is the Secretary of the Pitt Quadball (formerly known as Quidditch) team. She is also a Chaser and Seeker on the team. She was the Social Media Executive Director for Pitt Tonight, Pitt's student-run late-night talk show.

Conwell-Egan Catholic High School (CEC)

Delaney graduated as salutatorian from her 2020 graduating class. She excelled in honors and AP courses throughout her years at CEC,

Outside of academics, she was an active member of EAST Initiave, the Drama Club, and the junior varsity soccer team. As a member of EAST, she was selected for a national conference team three times, and in her senior year, she was selected for the organization's national Ambassador Team for the conference.
